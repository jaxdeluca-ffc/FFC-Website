Production
Incentives.

A state-by-state guide to film and television production tax credits, rebates, and grants across the United States. Know your options. Maximize your budget.

Tax Credits

Refundable or transferable credits that reduce tax liability. The most common incentive type, offered by 30+ states.

Rebates

Direct cash reimbursements from the state, usually paid within 90 days after principal photography wraps.

Grants

Discretionary awards from state film offices. Amounts and eligibility vary. No tax return filing required.

States Without Statewide Programs

Alaska, Florida, Idaho, Kansas, Michigan, New Hampshire, North Dakota, South Dakota, Vermont, Wyoming

Some of these states have local or municipal incentive programs. Florida has programs through Film Lauderdale, Miami-Dade, and North Miami.

Important

Incentive programs change frequently. This guide is for informational purposes and may not reflect the most current legislation. Always verify details with state film offices and qualified tax advisors before making production decisions. Data sourced from Entertainment Partners, Wrapbook, and GreenSlate.
