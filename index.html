About

An advocacy group dedicated to the future of film

The Future Film Coalition fosters audience demand, innovative policy and regulation, and revitalized entrepreneurial activity to support diverse artists and film professionals.

We believe that independent film is the lifeblood of cultural storytelling—and that the systems surrounding it must evolve to protect and empower the creators who drive it forward.

Mission
We work to transform the independent filmmaking ecosystem through policy advocacy, community engagement, and industry collaboration.
